MY PORTFOLIO

DANCING

Dancing as a hobby offers a joyful blend of physical fitness, mental well-being, and creative expression, serving as a powerful stress reliever and a way to connect with others, regardless of age or background, by translating emotions into rhythmic movements and steps, fostering coordination, confidence, and a sense of community through shared passion for music and movement.

ART & CRAFT

Art and craft as a hobby offers a fulfilling escape, allowing for self-expression, stress relief, and skill development through creating tangible objects, from sketching and painting to intricate paper or textile work, providing immense satisfaction and a meditative flow state for people of all ages. It's a versatile activity that encourages imagination, helps develop patience, and results in unique, personalized creations, making it a valuable way to connect with creativity beyond formal artistic skill.

CODING

Coding as a hobby offers a creative, puzzle-like challenge, turning ideas into reality through problem-solving, building apps, or automating tasks, providing immense satisfaction and continuous learning in a constantly evolving digital world, even with small, consistent efforts. It builds discipline and patience, fostering unique digital skills, though it can be frustrating when things don't work, making consistency and managing expectations key to enjoyment, whether it's a simple personal project or a path to new career opportunities.
